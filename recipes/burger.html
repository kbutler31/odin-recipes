Burger

what is this recipe?

This is a basic recipe for a hamburger. It's simple, but it's a great way to start learning how to make a hamburger. It's also a great way to start learning how to make a hamburger.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill for medium heat, and lightly oil the grill grate.
  2. whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
  3. add ground beef and bread crumbs to the egg mixture; mix well.
  4. Form ground beef into 1/2, 3/4-inch thick patties.
  5. place patties on the grill.
  6. cook 4 to 5 minutes on each side or until desired doneness.
  7. serve hot.
